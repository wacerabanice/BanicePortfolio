Financial Modeling


Building Microsoft Excel models, that forecasts a business’s financial performance into the future. The forecast is typically based on the company’s historical performance and assumptions about the future, and requires preparing an income statement, balance sheet, cash flow statement, and supporting schedules


Financial models are used to make decisions about:

  • Raising capital (debt and/or equity)
  • Making acquisitions (businesses and/or assets)
  • Budgeting and forecasting (planning for the years
  • Valuing a business



Credit Analysis


Assessing the creditworthiness of an individual or company to determine the likelihood that they will honor their financial obligations. I evaluate a borrower’s past financial and credit history to determine their financial health and their ability to repay credit advanced to them by a lender.

Credit Analysis involves:

  • Evaluating Credit Risk
  • Assessing Credit limits for existing customers
  • Analyzing Financial Data
